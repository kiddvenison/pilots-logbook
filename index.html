The Pilot's Logbook

A logbook of curated flights.

Track missions you've flown. Add notes. Drop in screenshots from the sim. Keep a journal. Build your own custom missions. All your data lives in a folder on your computer that you control.

Heads up: Your browser doesn't support the File System Access API, which this app uses to read and write your logbook folder. Use Chrome, Edge, Opera, or Brave for the full experience. Firefox and Safari won't work yet.
